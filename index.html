About the Project

The United Filipino League unites countless Filipinos as a community through their shared passion for basketball. This photo shoot aimed to capture the pure essence of flow—those moments when individuals are fully immersed in the sport they love most. I chose this team because their undeniable bond and impressive stats showcase their skills and dominance in the league. It’s incredible to capture the determination and teamwork that drive their hard-fought victories.When T.G.I.F takes the court, they’re completely locked in, with everything else fading into the background. These shots capture their journey—dynamic action moments, intense concentration, and celebrations.
